# Pizza-Sales
🚀 Pizza Sales Analysis Project | SQL + Power BI

I’m excited to share one of my recent data analytics projects where I worked on analyzing pizza sales data to uncover meaningful business insights.

In this project, I worked with multiple datasets including orders, order details, pizzas, and pizza types. The goal was to understand sales performance, customer preferences, and overall business trends.

🔍 Using SQL, I performed:

Data extraction and analysis using JOINs, GROUP BY, and aggregate functions
Calculated key metrics like total revenue, total orders, and total quantity
Identified top-selling pizzas and most popular sizes
Analyzed order distribution by time to find peak business hours
Explored category-wise performance and customer behavior

📊 Using Power BI, I:

Designed an interactive and user-friendly dashboard
Created KPI cards to highlight important metrics like Total Revenue, Total Orders, and Average Sales
Built visualizations such as bar charts, pie charts, donut charts, and column charts
Presented insights in a clear and visually appealing way

💡 Key Insights:

Certain pizza types contribute significantly more to overall revenue
Customer demand varies based on time and pizza category
Peak order hours play a crucial role in business performance

🎯 What I learned:

How to clean and transform raw data into structured format
Writing efficient SQL queries for business problem-solving
Creating impactful dashboards for better decision-making
Improving data storytelling and visualization skills

This project strengthened my understanding of data analytics and gave me hands-on experience in working with real-world datasets.
